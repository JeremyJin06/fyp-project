Phishing Learning Material

Learn about different phishing techniques and scenarios to protect yourself online.

  • Email Phishing: Deceptive emails that trick you into revealing personal information.
  • Spear Phishing: Targeted phishing attacks on specific individuals.
  • Clone Phishing: Duplicate emails with malicious links or attachments.
  • Whaling: Phishing attacks targeting high-profile individuals.
  • Voice Phishing (Vishing): Phone calls pretending to be from legitimate organizations.
  • Smishing (SMS Phishing): Text messages with malicious links or requests.
  • Social Media Phishing: Deceptive messages on social media platforms.
  • Website Spoofing: Fake websites that mimic legitimate ones to steal information.
